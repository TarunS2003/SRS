About Us

SRS Contractors: Elevating Construction Excellence

SRS Contractors is a dynamic and progressive renovation contractor dedicated to providing exceptional services for the construction, reconstruction, restoration, and redevelopment of civil infrastructure. With a focus on quality craftsmanship, innovative solutions, and meticulous project management, we have quickly established ourselves as a trusted name in the industry.

Our History: Building a Legacy of Success

Although a newly registered company, SRS Contractors boasts a rich legacy of expertise and experience. Previously operating under the esteemed names of Shree Shyam Constructions and PR Constrowell, we have amassed a wealth of knowledge and achievements in the field. Our transition to SRS Contractors signifies a new chapter in our journey, where we continue to set new standards of excellence.

Unparalleled Expertise: The Artisans of Construction

At SRS Contractors, we take pride in our dedicated team of professionals who are masters of their craft. Our diverse workforce includes supervisors, electricians, painters, plumbers, masons, carpenters, and over 100 skilled workers, each possessing vast experience in their respective fields. With their unparalleled expertise, we consistently deliver outstanding results on every project we undertake.

Exceeding Expectations: Transforming Spaces

With an unwavering commitment to our clients' satisfaction, we have had the privilege of transforming numerous structures, including housing institutions, corporate companies, and educational establishments. From meticulously planned renovations to complete overhauls, we approach each project with a systematic and scientific methodology, ensuring the highest level of quality at every stage.

Innovation and Professionalism: Redefining the Sector

At SRS Contractors, we firmly believe in pushing boundaries and revolutionizing the construction industry.
